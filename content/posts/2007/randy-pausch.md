Randy Pausch's inspirational last [lecture](http://video.google.com/videoplay?docid=362421849901825950&hl=en). Also see [website](http://www.cs.cmu.edu/%7Epausch/). Randy Pausch is a professor at Carnegie Melon University which was recently diagnosed with pancreatic cancer (operated on, but the cancer returned), and knows he has only a few months to live. What wisdom would you impart to future generations if you were in this position? The lecture's title is "Really Achieving Your Childhood Dreams".
