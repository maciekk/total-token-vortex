title: '''hot pixels'' in digital cameras'

After playing around with my new Nikon D90, I noticed that some of my dark pictures have a red spot near upper right corner. It turns out this is a "hot pixel". Further research led me to the following interesting pages:

* <http://www.shutterasia.com/forum/showthread.php?s=15d8e681214727cd9818820599fc1f45&t=24484>
* [pixelfixer](http://www.pixelfixer.org/) - a tool for filtering out hot pixels from images
* [good intro to hot pixels](http://webpages.charter.net/bbiggers/DCExperiments/html/hot_pixels.html#)
