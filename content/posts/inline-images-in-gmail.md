title: inline images in Gmail
---

Officially inserting inline images in Gmail is not possible. However, there is a [workaround](http://groups.google.com/group/Gmail-Help-Composing-Messages-en/msg/84a76f44a7760e05):
> Open Google Docs in a new window, open a Word Doc. Insert the picture
> you want from your computer. This converts it into rich text format.
> Now you can click on the picture in Google Docs, Copy it, then paste
> it into your gmail compose screen.
